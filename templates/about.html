About TextTools

TextTools is a small tool made for the purpose of manipulating text according to the given options.

Simply, check the required task and you are one button away from analyzing your text. You can check multiple tasks at a time.

This app is built on Django, just for the purpose of learning about concepts of views, urls and templates. Thanks to CodeWithHarry.

Tasks :

  • • Remove Punctuations
  • • Remove Numbers
  • • Remove Newlines
  • • Remove Extra Spaces
  • • Capitalize Text
  • • Reverse Text
  • • ALL IN ONE CLICK
